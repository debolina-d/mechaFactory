About Us

"We don't just design. We deliver."

Mecha Factory is a product design and consultancy company offering complete OEM and ODM solutions. We specialize in 3D/2D Design, rapid prototyping, CNC engraving, laser cutting, and Design training.

We proudly serve startups, researchers, students, makers, and manufacturers, empowering innovation at every stage.
