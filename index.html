About Us

Engineering your ideas into reality with precision and purpose.

Mecha Factory is India’s premier OEM and ODM design consultancy, offering complete product development solutions tailored to your needs. From the first sketch to final manufacturing, we specialize in transforming innovative concepts into fully functional, production-ready designs.

With a cutting-edge R&D studio and a passionate team of design engineers, we deliver high-quality 3D and 2D modeling, 3D printing, CNC engraving, laser cutting, and technical training in design and reverse engineering.

Our focus is precision, speed, and collaboration. Whether you’re a startup building your first prototype or a business scaling up production, Mecha Factory ensures efficient, reliable, and scalable solutions — powered by experience and technology.
